# PRODUCT.md: Snooty Beta Gallery

register: brand

## What this is
The launch keynote and companion demo pages for Snooty (hisisnooty.com), a two-sided beauty marketplace where clients discover and book beauty professionals by the look (the finished work), not the business name. This repo is presentation-grade: a presenter deck (`deck-preview.html`) projected in a room, plus phone-facing demo stops (discover, look, profile, booking, messages, dashboard) that every audience phone follows live via `shared/sync.js`.

## Users
- **In the room:** working stylists and braiders (Fayetteville, NC and nearby). Visual professionals; they judge presentation quality instantly and photograph what impresses them. They currently run their business on Instagram DMs, texts, a notes app, and Square.
- **At the podium:** the founder, presenting solo with a clicker.

## Purpose
Convert the room into founding beta stylists. The deck sells belief, not features: clients find your work, book it with an enforced deposit, and the admin runs itself.

## Tone
Calm, premium, editorial. Confident enough to hold silence. Warm, never corporate. Money is discussed with total transparency (one real $220 booking, dissected).

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS pitch decks, bullet lists, gradient-text startup slides, bouncy easing, cold tech-blue anything, Instagram-clone aesthetics.

## Strategic principles
- The projector is the presentation; the phones are proof.
- Everything on screen must look like it shipped from the Snooty app itself (same tokens as the real product).
- Numbers shown must match the seeded booking ($220 service, $44 deposit, $209 kept) and the decided model ($50/yr Annual Listing Fee, 5% of appointment value collected from the deposit).
