Booking

The flow a client uses to book and pay a deposit.

The moment she books, it's real — a deposit charged instantly, a slot only you marked as open, a price that's exactly what you set.

  • Deposit, charged now. Refunds automatically if you cancel; you keep it if she no-shows.
  • Real availability, enforced. Only your open times ever show — no double-booking.
  • Your price, exactly. Nothing renegotiated in a DM.
  • Service picked first. Both sides know exactly what's being booked, before a time is ever chosen.

What We Need From You

Five things, as a founding beta stylist.

Your feedback shapes the product before anyone else sees it.

  • Use your real business. Real services, prices, availability, portfolio — no placeholders.
  • Bring real clients, quietly. Personal texts to people you trust — not a public post yet.
  • Report bugs immediately. What you tried, what happened, what screen — even the small stuff.
  • Tell us what's confusing. Not every issue is a bug. Those conversations become our biggest fixes.
  • Expect it to change. That's what this stage is for.

Save Snooty's contact now — feedback should always be one text away.
